Our Mission

To promote and support the art of juggling in Philadelphia and beyond through education, community building, and collaboration with local organizations.

Our Vision

To enhance the general perception and awareness of juggling in the greater Philadelphia area through the facilitation of information-sharing, resources and networking in-person as well as virtually.
